Portrayal of Sepore14

Sepore14 is the 14th iteration of Sepore (obviously), the most recent of the iterations, as well as the most well known to the public. He is characterized as overzealous and cocky, with a load of stupidity, and a hint of excellence. 014 is generally described by the public as an asshole, an idiot, or (rarely) misunderstood. While he has openly acknowledged these descriptions to be true in certain scenarios, he is typically indifferent towards these adjectives to his character. Sepore14 always pushes for what's important in any given circumstance, often times disregarding others' opinions on the matter. He is truly indifferent towards the world and its problems, only caring in the slightest if it has any effect on him or his sympathies. Compared to other Sepores, 014 has spoken of himself as 'inconsistent' as opposed to the rest.
